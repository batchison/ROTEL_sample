Section 4 Quizzes and Exams

Three quizzes, worth 15 points each, will be administered during the semester, with the lowest quiz being dropped from the student's overall point total.

Four exams will be administered throughout the semester, along with a final exam. Each in-class exam is worth 45 points. Each student's lowest in-class exam score will be replaced with the average of their other three in-class exams.

The final exam will be worth 75 course points. The instructor reserves the right to make up to 50 final exam points consist of an oral component, administered via Zoom. All other assignments will be administered via Blackboard.

Calculators, tablets, cell phones, or any other devices other than the student's primary laptop will not be allowed for quizzes or exams, and credit will not be awarded for any answer that does not include sufficient work. Makeup or late assignments will not be permitted under any circumstances.
