Section 5 Attendance and Participation

Attendance will not be taken. Students who have been observed by the instructor as either frequently absent or late to class or non-participatory may be subject to point deductions from their overall course score. Students are responsible for obtaining lecture notes from any missed classes. At any point during the semester, students may be asked to provide proper documentation to the Office of Student Affairs, justifying any absence from the University.

Successful completion of this course, especially remotely, requires active participation on the part of the student. In an effort to promote classroom engagement and motivate student success, every effort on the part of the instructor will be centered around encouraging active participation in a supportive learning environment. Consequently, students will be expected to attend class on time, with cameras on, and ready to contribute. Any student who cannot meet these expectations for whatever reason must notify the instructor formally via email with their concerns.
